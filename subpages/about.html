About

Game Hub Started up 6th of October 2022 after seeing how the pandemic in 2019 sky-rocketed the demand for games. People where stuck at home, and got more into gaming due to this.

Although platforms like Steam, GOG, Epic Games etc. exist, we still believe there is room for a online store that focuses on indie-games.

Indie-games can be just as fun, if not more, than games made by billion-dollar companies. We focus on publishing games that the player brings feedback to during the development phase.

We can proudly say that Game Hub offer games made by gamers.
