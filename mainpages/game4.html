Furious


Prove that the The Widowmaker actually exist!


Furious is a game where you join a team of explorers in the deep African jungle to find "The Widowmaker" as the local villagers calls it. People have witnessed the half-cat/half-gorilla looking monster slaughtering not only other animals, but also humans getting to close to it. Hunters that have tried tracking it down, have all disappered, thereby the name.

Your mission is to find The Widowmaker. The main objective is to document that it actually exist. But beware, this creature is rumoured to being extremely hostile.
